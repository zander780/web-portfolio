Why I Like Nahko

Nahko Bear is the lead singer of Nahko And Medicince For The People. With a wordly-folk style music, the songs range from love songs to songs about indigenous activism, to an homage of the Grandfather Teachings (7 Feathers). Nahko has a down-to-earth style and approaches the world with the same teachings he shares in his music. Nahko is a true storyteller.

About Nahko And The Medicine People

Nahko was adopted and raised by a conservative caucasian family in Oregon. In his later teen years and as a young adult, Nahko went on a journey of self-discovery and purpose because he learned about his Puerto Rican, Indigenous, and Filipino ancestry. His music reflects Nahko's journey, discoveries, and insights and includes social and environmental advocacy.

Nahko And Medicine For The People released their debut album in 2011, titled 'On the Verge'. 'Dark As Night', released in 2013, was the album that sparked the growth of the band's popularity. The album release led to festival spots and was featured in the Top Ten Billboard Heatseekers Chart.
